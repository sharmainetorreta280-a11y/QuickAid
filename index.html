About the Study

This prototype was developed to organize basic first aid information for minor wounds in one accessible platform. It provides immediate first aid guidance and, when available, optional natural remedies commonly associated with skin care and soothing effects. The system focuses on minor wounds only and promotes safe, informed, and practical responses during non-emergency situations.
